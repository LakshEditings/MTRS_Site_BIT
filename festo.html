Overview

About FESTO Center of Excellence

The FESTO Center of Excellence is a state-of-the-art facility designed to provide hands-on experience with industrial automation systems. This center bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical industrial applications, enabling students to understand real-world manufacturing processes and Industry 4.0 concepts.

Our facility features complete production line stations that simulate actual industrial environments, allowing students to gain practical experience in pneumatic and hydraulic systems, PLC programming, sensor integration, and complete factory automation solutions.
