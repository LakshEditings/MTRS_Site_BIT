ABB Industrial Automation Center of Excellence

Overview

The ABB Center of Excellence focuses on power systems, robotics, and industrial automation solutions. It provides comprehensive training on ABB's cutting-edge technologies and systems.

Key Features

Industrial Robots
ABB IRB series robotic arms
Power Electronics
Variable frequency drives and inverters
Motion Control
Servo systems and motion controllers
HMI Systems
Human-machine interface solutions
SCADA Systems
Supervisory control and data acquisition
Safety Systems
Industrial safety and protection devices

Applications

Students learn to program and operate industrial robots, design power distribution systems, implement safety protocols, and develop complete automation solutions for manufacturing environments.

SEW EURODRIVE Drives Center of Excellence

Overview

The SEW EURODRIVE Center specializes in drive technology, gear motors, and motion control systems. It offers comprehensive training on drive solutions for various industrial applications.

Key Features

Gear Motors
Various types of gear motor systems
Variable Frequency Drives
Speed control and energy efficiency
Servo Systems
Precision positioning and control
Decentralized Drives
Distributed drive solutions
Safety Technology
Safe motion and drive protection
Energy Management
Energy-efficient drive solutions

Learning Outcomes

Students gain expertise in drive technology, learn to select appropriate drive solutions for different applications, understand energy efficiency principles, and develop skills in drive system integration and maintenance.

Drone & 3D Printing Center of Excellence

Overview

This cutting-edge center focuses on emerging technologies in unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and additive manufacturing. It provides hands-on experience with the latest drone technology and 3D printing systems.

Key Features

Quadcopter Systems
Multi-rotor drone platforms
FDM 3D Printers
Fused deposition modeling systems
SLA 3D Printers
Stereolithography printing technology
Flight Controllers
Autopilot and navigation systems
CAD/CAM Software
Design and manufacturing software
Material Testing
3D printing material analysis

Applications

Students learn drone assembly, programming, and operation for various applications including surveillance, mapping, and delivery systems. They also gain expertise in 3D modeling, printing processes, post-processing techniques, and rapid prototyping for product development.

Mechatronics Laboratory

Overview

The Mechatronics Laboratory is designed to provide integrated learning experiences combining mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering principles. It serves as the foundation for understanding mechatronic system design and integration.

Equipment & Systems

Sensor Systems
Various sensors for measurement and control
Actuator Systems
Motors, pneumatics, and hydraulics
Microcontrollers
Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and PIC systems
Data Acquisition
Real-time data collection systems
Control Systems
Feedback control implementation
Mechanical Systems
Linkages, gears, and transmission systems

Learning Objectives

Students develop skills in system integration, control algorithm implementation, sensor interfacing, and mechatronic system design. The lab emphasizes hands-on learning through project-based activities and real-world problem solving.

Computer Laboratory

Overview

The Computer Laboratory provides a comprehensive environment for software development, simulation, and computational analysis essential for mechatronic system design and implementation.

Software & Tools

MATLAB/Simulink
System modeling and simulation
SolidWorks
3D CAD modeling and analysis
LabVIEW
Virtual instrumentation platform
Programming Languages
C/C++, Python, and embedded programming
PLC Programming
Industrial automation programming
Simulation Software
System dynamics and control simulation

Capabilities

The lab enables students to develop programming skills, perform system simulations, design control algorithms, create 3D models, and analyze mechatronic systems before physical implementation. It serves as a digital twin environment for real-world applications.
